GOT
QUESTIONS?

Do I need to be fit to start CrossFit?

+

Not at all. CrossFit is for everyone regardless of fitness level. Every workout can be scaled and modified to meet you where you are. Our coaches guide beginners through fundamentals before jumping into group classes.

What should I bring to my first session?

+

Comfortable workout clothes, indoor training shoes, a water bottle, and a towel. We supply all the equipment. Just bring your energy and willingness to learn.

How does the intro program work?

+

Book an introductory session and tour where you'll meet a coach, view the gym, discuss your goals, and learn about our facility and what we offer. From there we'll build a plan to get you started.

Can I put my membership on hold?

+

Yes. Members may place their membership on hold twice yearly for up to 3 consecutive months each time, with a minimum of 2 business days notice required.

Do you offer drop-in sessions for visitors?

+

Yes, drop-ins are $29 per class. If you are visiting Auckland and have CrossFit experience, you are welcome to join any class on our timetable.
