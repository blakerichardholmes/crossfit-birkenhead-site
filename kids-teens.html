The Program

BUILT FOR
YOUNG ATHLETES

Our Kids & Teens Fitness Program uses proven CrossFit methodology to combine functional workouts with skill development, all wrapped in games, play, and friendly challenges.

Every session is tailored to ability, so whether your child is brand-new to exercise or already plays a sport, they'll be coached with the right scaling and encouragement.

The goal isn't to create gym rats. It's to teach young people how to move well, move often, and enjoy the process. Habits that last a lifetime.

What's Included

  • → Warm-ups and mobility designed for growing bodies
  • → Fundamental movement patterns (squat, hinge, push, pull, carry)
  • → Basic gymnastics & bodyweight skill work
  • → Team games and friendly challenges
  • → Small groups, constant coaching, zero judgement

Two Classes

FIND YOUR
AGE GROUP

Two separate classes, each with programming suited to where young athletes are in their development.

Ages 8–11

Kids Class

Foundational Movement

Packed with games, obstacle-style workouts, and fun skill challenges. We focus on coordination, balance, listening, and teamwork, setting young athletes up with great habits, early.

Ages 12–15

Teens Class

Strength & Skill Progression

A step up in technique and intensity. Teens learn proper lifting mechanics, progress through gymnastics skills, and build genuine strength, all with the right load and scaling for their age.

Our Aims

MORE THAN
A WORKOUT

What young athletes walk away with, week after week.

01

Functional Movement

Learn the squat, hinge, push, pull, and carry patterns that support every sport, and every day of life.

02

Self-Confidence

There's nothing like doing something you didn't think you could. Confidence grows rep by rep, class by class.

03

Coordination & Strength

Develop body awareness, balance, and age-appropriate strength: the building blocks for every sport.

04

Focus & Resilience

Discipline and persistence are trainable. Kids learn to stick with tough tasks and bounce back from setbacks.

05

A Lifelong Love of Movement

The real goal: young people who genuinely enjoy being active, long after they leave our program.

06

Friendship & Belonging

Training alongside peers builds community. Kids leave with training partners, teammates, and real friendships.
